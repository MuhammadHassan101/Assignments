  2. Qorma

    3. Ingredients

    • Meat (chicken, mutton, or beef)
    • Onions (fried golden brown)
    • Yogurt
    • Ginger-garlic paste
    • Tomatoes (optional, some versions skip them)
    • Cooking oil or ghee
    • Whole spices (bay leaves, cloves, cinnamon, cardamom, black pepper)
    • Ground spices (red chili powder, turmeric, coriander powder, garam masala)
    • Salt
    • Green chilies
    • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)
    • Fresh ginger slices (for garnish)
    • Water or stock (for gravy)
  3. Nihari

    4. Ingredients

    • Meat (beef or mutton, with bones/marrow if possible)
    • Onions
    • Ginger-garlic paste
    • Flour (atta, for thickening)
    • Spices (red chili, turmeric, coriander, cumin, garam masala, etc.)
    • Oil or ghee
    • Water/stock
    • fresh ginger slices
    • green chilies
    • coriander
    • lemon wedges
  4. Karahi

    5. Ingredients

    • Meat (chicken or mutton)
    • Tomatoes (lots, the base of karahi)
    • Ginger-garlic paste
    • Green chilies
    • Fresh coriander leaves
    • Spices (red chili, turmeric, coriander powder, garam masala, salt)
    • Oil or ghee
    • Fresh ginger (for garnish)
